Common Earthing Methods

Plate Earthing

Copper or GI plate buried vertically in soil.

  • Depth: ~3 meters
  • Used in buildings

Pipe Earthing

Most common and economical method.

  • GI pipe with holes
  • Good for domestic loads

Rod Earthing

Copper/GI rod driven into ground.

  • Easy installation
  • Used where space is limited

Chemical Earthing

Low resistance, maintenance-free system.

  • Uses conductive compounds
  • Stable resistance
